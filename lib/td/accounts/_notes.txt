*** Trigger OCO ***
Buy 5 shares of XYZ at a Limit price of $14.97 good for the Day.  Once filled, 2 sell orders are 
immediately sent: Sell 5 shares of XYZ at a Limit price of $15.27 and Sell 5 shares of XYZ with a 
Stop order where the stop price is $11.27.  When one of the sell order fills, the other order is 
immediately cancelled.  Both Sell orders are Good till Cancel.  Also known as a 1st Trigger OCO order.
